Premium Facilities

State-of-the-art aquatic centers designed for champions.

Aquatic Center

50m Olympic-Grade Pool

Our pride and joy is a fully equipped, temperature-controlled 50-meter Olympic pool. Designed for both competitive swim training and recreational use, it features state-of-the-art wave disruption lines that mimic professional race conditions.

Temperature Controlled

Maintained at an optimal 26-28°C for performance swimming year-round.

Advanced Filtration

We use UV and Ozone purification systems for the cleanest, most skin-friendly water.

Strength & Conditioning

High-Performance Gym

A swimmer's power is built on land. Our facility includes a fully-equipped gymnasium tailored specifically for dryland strength training. It provides everything necessary to build core stability, explosive power, and endurance.

Free Weights & Machines

A comprehensive setup. We focus on exercises that translate directly into water propulsion.

Cardio Zone

Treadmills, rowing machines, and assault bikes for aerobic capacity conditioning.

Wellness

Dedicated Recovery Zone

Recovery is just as important as the training itself. Aqua Haven provides dedicated wellness amenities to help athletes recover faster, prevent injuries, and mentally unwind after intense sessions.

Sauna & Steam

Relax the muscles, improve circulation, and clear the respiratory system.

Sports Massage Therapy

In-house therapists available for deep tissue and sports recovery massages.
