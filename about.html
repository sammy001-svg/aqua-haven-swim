Our Story

Dedicated to excellence in aquatic training and water safety since 2015.

Who We Are

Pioneering Aquatic Excellence in Nanyuki

Aqua Haven Swim Ke was founded on a singular vision: to create a holistic training environment where swimmers of all levels—from beginners taking their first splash to elite athletes preparing for competition—can achieve their ultimate potential.

Located at the beautiful Esiankiki resort in Nanyuki, our facility combines an Olympic-grade swimming pool with state-of-the-art dryland strength and conditioning amenities. We recognized that true athletic performance requires a synergy of disciplines, which is why we built a center that addresses every aspect of a swimmer's development.

Our team of dedicated coaches and professionals are passionate about aquatic sports. We don't just teach swimming; we instill confidence, discipline, and a lifelong love for the water.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional swimming instruction and comprehensive athletic training in a safe, inspiring, and elite facility, empowering individuals to achieve their personal and competitive goals.

Our Vision

To be the premier aquatic and fitness destination in East Africa, recognized for cultivating world-class talent and fostering an inclusive community of health, wellness, and excellence.
